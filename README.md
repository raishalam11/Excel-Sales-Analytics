# 🚀 Sales & Finance Analytics Report | Excel & Data Modeling Project

I recently worked on a data analytics project focused on building Sales and Finance performance reports to help businesses gain deeper insights into their operations and make data-driven decisions.

# 📊 Sales Analytics Report

The objective of this report was to analyze sales performance and provide valuable insights into customer and market behavior.

## Key highlights of the report include:
- Customer Performance Report – Evaluates how different customers contribute to overall sales and identifies high-value customers.
- Market Performance vs Sales Target – A detailed comparison of actual sales performance against predefined targets across markets.

These insights help businesses monitor sales activities, identify key performance indicators (KPIs), determine effective discount strategies, support negotiations with customers, and explore potential expansion opportunities in high-performing markets.

# 📈 Finance Analytics Report

In addition to sales analysis, I also developed Profit & Loss (P&L) reports to evaluate financial performance and support strategic decision-making.

## The financial reports include:
- P&L Report by Fiscal Year and Month – Tracks financial performance over time.
- P&L Report by Markets – Provides visibility into profitability across different regions.

These reports help organizations benchmark their performance against previous periods, support budgeting and forecasting, and communicate financial insights effectively to stakeholders.

- Creating calculated columns using DAX
